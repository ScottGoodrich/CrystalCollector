Crystal Collector

You will be given a random target number at the start of the game.
Clicking on the four crystals below will add a certain amount of points to your score.
To win the game, match your points to the target number without going over!
The point value of each crystal will remain hidden until it is clicked.
All numbers are reset at the start of each game.
Good luck!
